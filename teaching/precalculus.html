Overview

Precalculus is a course designed to prepare students for the study of calculus. It covers a wide range of mathematical concepts and skills that are essential for success in calculus, including functions, trigonometry, and analytic geometry.

Topics

  • Representation of complex numbers
  • Functions and their properties
  • Sequences and series
  • Polynomial and rational functions
  • Exponential and logarithmic functions
  • Trigonometric functions
  • Vectors and Matrcies
