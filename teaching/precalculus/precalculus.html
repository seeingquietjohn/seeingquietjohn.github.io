Overview

One last review before calculus on everything you need to know for calculus. In other words,
it's okay if you've failed every single math class so far; this is your opportunity to
redeem yourself. Plus, if you're here, you're nearly at the end of the tunnel (hurray!).

Prerequisites

  • High school algebra and elementary geometry/trigonometry

Topics

  • Representation of complex numbers
  • Functions and their properties
  • Sequences and series
  • Polynomial and rational functions
  • Exponential and logarithmic functions
  • Trigonometric functions
  • Vectors and Matrcies

Suggested Texts

  • Precalculus with Unit Circle Trigonometry, 4th edition, by David Cohen
  • Calculus, any edition, by James Stewart
