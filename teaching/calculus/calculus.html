Overview

An introduction to continuous functions, limits, derivatives, and integrals.

Prerequisites

  • High school algebra, geometry/trigonometry, and a study of basic algebraic and transcendental functions

Topics

  • Limits and continuity
  • Differentiation and derivatives
  • Integrals
  • The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
  • Differential equations
  • Parametric representation of curves
  • Infinite sequences and series

Suggested Texts

  • Calculus, any edition, by James Stewart
  • Calculus, 4th edition, by Michael Spivak
