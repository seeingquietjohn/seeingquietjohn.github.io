1. Making a Prediction

We look at the past, and then make a guess about the future. The most general guess we can make is a probability distribution over future events.

Here is a centered block equation:

\[ I[X_{t+1}^{\infty}; X_{-\infty}^t] \geq I[X_{t+1}^{\infty}; \sigma(X_{-\infty}^t)] \]
